About me and Chan-chan

Artist bio and Chan-chan story.

Hi! Im Rocio, an illustrator from Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I draw since I was a little child and I have always loved pigs. There is something about their snouts, ears and curly tails that makes them so adorable and cute.

Chan-chan was born from this two passions of mine: drawing and pigs. I started making piggy t-shirts, to promote them I used to draw a new pig almost every day. That´s how I ended up having a huge gallery of piggy characters.

Right now I´m looking for partners to make more cute products with my beloved piggies on them, Are you interested? Do you want to ask me anything? I would love to hear from you!
